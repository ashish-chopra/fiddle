What is Fiddle, btw?
We always dreamt of working together on ideas and bringing its fruits to the world, however we think world was not yet ready for it :) But now, with an excuse to celebrate Naseem’s first medal of recognition, fiddle is our first entry to the wall of fame.

Actually, fiddle is our lifebook to pin places which we’ve ever visited in our lifetime, all around the world & the universe (I hope somebody will visit to Mars soon :p). We’ve recorded those events with some nostalgic instances that we'll cherish whenever we open this book again and again in time.

And last but not the least, A big cheers for Naseem for realizing fiddle and make his boss shine in office and to Shivam for finding this opportunity for Naseem!!

Guys it's dedicated to you!!
